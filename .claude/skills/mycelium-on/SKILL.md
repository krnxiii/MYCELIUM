Enable MYCELIUM knowledge graph access (read + write).

Steps:
1. Run: `mkdir -p ~/.mycelium && touch ~/.mycelium/.read_enabled ~/.mycelium/.write_enabled`
2. Confirm to user: "MYCELIUM read+write enabled."
